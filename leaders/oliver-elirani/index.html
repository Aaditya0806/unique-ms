About Unique

Oliver has more than 13 years of institutional and entrepreneurial experiences in different industries and practices, working for and with leading consulting firms, corporations, startups, investors and governmental entities across the Middle East and Africa region. In 2021 and 2022, Oliver was recognized as one of top 50 government consultants in the world by The Consulting Report.

Prior toUnique Management Services , Oliver worked at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PWC) Middle East where he served in Beirut, Riyadh, and Dubai offices across different practices, from managing the Agribusiness practice to Consumer Industrial Products & Services (CIPS), Government consulting and Deals Transaction services. He led and delivered some of the highest impact engagements across the region and worked extensively on Saudi Arabia Vision 2030 closely with leading ministries, authorities and government-backed funds. He also served as the deputy CEO for Badia farms UAE, the first high tech vertical farm in the Middle east. Prior to PwC, Oliver managed credit portfolios of $1 Bln for two leading banks, analyzing, monitoring and making recommendations for commercial opportunities across major industries in line with the banks investment strategy.
